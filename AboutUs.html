About Us

Diazepam café was founded in 2009 by Adib. The café started as a small local business which only sold common ice cream flavors such as chocolate, vanilla and almond.  It has struggled at first but through intensive research and development, they understood the needs of flavors in Singapore. By achieving the necessity of customers tastes in ice cream, Diazepam Café has gradually grown up and expanding into 5 outlets in Singapore. Now Diazepam Café has been renown through its unique and exotic ice cream flavors. Diazepam café is also known for offering 4 ice cream type products which include ice creams, ice cream cakes, frozen yogurt and ice cream milkshakes. In only 3 months, it has managed to sell over a million Cookies and Cream Ice-Cream, which is also its signature ice-cream. Ice-creams are always serve at its best quality in Diazepam café.
