Exchange and Returns

1) Delivery will occur once the $5 payment has been made if total cost of products is lower than $50

2) If products melt during the delivery process, product will be re-made and delivered as soon as possible

3) If product is not delivered according to the estimated time, then product will be offered for free

4) There will be no exchange of product if user has consumed it

5) Upon delivery, you have 1 day for exchanging the product if it was a wrong delivery or issues were found on the product

6) If Exchange and Return is required, please contact our number or come by the company

7) There will be no return in cash if customer purchase the product. Therefore it can only be exchange
